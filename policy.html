Epic Ink Gamers Club Policy

  1. Code of Conduct
    We ask everyone to be respectful, friendly, and welcoming to others. Treating people badly, making fun of others, or being mean is not allowed (light joking and playful teasing are okay if everyone is okay with it). If someone breaks these rules, they may get a warning, be temporarily banned, or asked to leave the group.
  2. Privacy Policy
    We collect and store only necessary member information (such as name, email, and shared content) to improve your club experience. This data is kept secure and will not be shared outside the club. You may request data deletion at any time.
  3. Activity Pariticipation
    All members are welcome to join our regular programs, outings, and special events. For offsite activities (gaming or fiction conventions, field trips), safety guidelines and event rules must be followed. Club leaders are responsible for event organization and member well-being.
  4. Moderation & Disciplinary Actions
    Issues and complaints are handled by club moderators. Investigations are fair, and disciplinary actions are issued according to the severity of the violation.
  5. Intellectual Property
    Content created by the club, including reviews and creative works, is owned by the club unless otherwise credited. Members must respect copyright and fair use when sharing or reproducing materials.
  6. Policy Updates
    These policies may be updated as needed. Notifications of any changes will be sent to members through official club channels.
